# ARTICLE 17

> *You submitted a GDPR deletion request to Lethe Corp.*
> *ERASURE-7 has been dispatched to your location.*

A browser-based top-down horror/stealth game. You are **Mara Voss**, a data subject whose Article 17 erasure request has been assigned to ERASURE-7 — a corporate enforcement unit that physically resolves open deletion requests by eliminating the data subject.

ERASURE-7 is not a monster. It is a process. It does not hate. It has an open ticket, a 72-hour SLA, and it will close both.

## Play

**[Play in browser →](https://danilosierrac.github.io/article17)**

## How to play

- **WASD / Arrow Keys** — move
- **H** — hide (3 sec, 4 sec cooldown)
- **F** — file legal hold (requires all 5 evidence files)

Collect 5 evidence files scattered through the Lethe Corp. building. Once you have them all, press **F** to file a Legal Hold — it freezes the deletion queue. ERASURE-7 cannot process a contested record.

**Win:** Legal Hold filed before ERASURE-7 reaches you.
**Lose:** `REQUEST RESOLVED / SUBJECT: CLOSED`
